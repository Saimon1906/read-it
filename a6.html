Introduction

In today's globalized world, English has become the most widely spoken international language. Learning English brings many advantages, especially for students and professionals. It is often called the "language of opportunity," as it helps people connect with others from different countries.

Education, Science, and Technology

Firstly, English is the main language of higher education, science, and technology. Most academic journals, research papers, and modern inventions are published in English.

Therefore, mastering the language allows learners to access a wide range of knowledge and stay updated with global developments.

Secondly, English is important for career growth. Many multinational companies require employees to communicate in English with clients and colleagues. For instance, in countries like Bangladesh, knowing English increases the chances of getting better jobs and promotions.
